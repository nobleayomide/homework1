Potato Porridge

Ingredients Required

  • potatoes
  • palm oil
  • crayfish
  • seasoning
  • peppermix
  • onions
  • salt
  • ugu

Preparation steps

Peel and slice potatoes and rinse with water

Put water on fire and add your sliced potatoes

Add your onions and salt

Allow to boil till its very soft

Add your palm oil

Add your blended peppermix

Add your seasoning and crayfish

cover the pot and leave for about 8-10 minutes

stir till it becomes moderate

Add your ugu leafs

Prepare potato porridge
